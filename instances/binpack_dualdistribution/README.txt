This archive contains the problem instances used in the following paper.

Burke E. K., Hyde M. R. and Kendall G. 2010. "Providing a Memory Mechanism to Enhance the Evolutionary Design of Heuristics". In Proceedings of the IEEE World Congress on Computational Intelligence (CEC 2010). July 18-23 2010, Barcelona, Spain, pp 3883--3890

They contain items drawn from gaussian distributions, and some instances contain items drawn from two distributions. See the paper for details.
